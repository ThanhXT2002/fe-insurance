Our Story

Founded in 1998, SecureGuard Insurance began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable insurance coverage that truly protects our customers when they need it most. What started as a local agency has grown into a trusted national provider, serving over 2.5 million customers across the country.

Our commitment to innovation and customer service has driven us to develop cutting-edge digital solutions while maintaining the personal touch that sets us apart. We believe insurance should be simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

Today, we continue to evolve and adapt to meet the changing needs of our customers, offering comprehensive coverage options from traditional policies to emerging risks like cybersecurity threats.
